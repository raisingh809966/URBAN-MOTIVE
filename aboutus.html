Why only We

At Urban Motive, we believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, wholesome, and sustainably grown food. That's why we're committed to revolutionizing the way people shop for groceries by delivering natural farm products right to their doorstep.


Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between organic food and consumers by delivering farm-fresh products directly from our fields to your home. Tired of the long supply chains and lack of transparency in the food industry, we decided to take matters into our own hands. We established our own farms where we follow sustainable farming practices to cultivate the finest fruits, vegetables, grains, and more.
